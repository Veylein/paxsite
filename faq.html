Pax FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Pax free?

Yes. 100% free. No premium. No paid boosts.

How do I get pets?

Use the pet-based commands. For extended help use P!help.

How do achievements work?

Achievements unlock automatically when you meet the requirements.

Is Pax safe?

Yes. Pax stores only gameplay data and respects user privacy.

How do I report bugs?

Join the support server or open an issue on GitHub.
