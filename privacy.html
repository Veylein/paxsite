Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This policy explains what information Pax collects and how it is used, stored, and protected.

Privacy Policy

Pax collects the following types of information:

  • Discord User ID, username, and discriminator
  • Server ID and name where Pax is used
  • Bot-specific data such as pets, achievements, chi, and game progress

We do not collect passwords, personal emails, or any sensitive financial information.

Data collected is used solely to provide and improve the Pax experience, including:

  • Tracking game progression, pets, and achievements
  • Enabling cross-server stats and leaderboards
  • Personalizing in-bot messages and notifications

No data is sold or shared with third parties.

User data is stored securely as long as the user interacts with Pax. Inactive accounts may have data archived or deleted after extended inactivity, at the discretion of the developers.

Pax developers employ best practices for security, including encrypted storage and secure servers. However, no method of data transmission over the internet can be 100% secure. By using Pax, you accept this risk.

You have the right to request data deletion or export. To do so, contact Pax support through the official server or email provided on the site.

Pax may integrate with third-party services such as Discord APIs. These services have their own privacy policies. Pax does not share user data beyond what is necessary to interact with these services.

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of Pax constitutes acceptance of updated policies.
